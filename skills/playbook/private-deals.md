# Private / Venture Deals — DD Framework (separate track)

A framework for **private or venture investments** (a startup round, a pre-IPO SPV) — kept
*separate* from the tradable public-equity pipeline, since liquidity, diligence, and risk differ.
Generic checklist; keep any specific deal in a git-ignored `skills/private/` file, never the public
repo.

## Why it's a separate track

Private deals are illiquid, information-poor, and binary. Don't force them through the quant/levels
pipeline (no chart). Judge on diligence and structure.

## Diligence checklist

- **Team** — founder track record, domain fit, incentives, cap-table sanity.
- **Market** — real TAM and *why now*; is the wave inflecting (`skills/edge/thematic-waves.md`)?
- **Product / moat** — what's defensible; who the real competitors are.
- **Traction** — revenue/usage, retention, pipeline; evidence over narrative.
- **Terms** — valuation vs. comparables, dilution, liquidation preference, board/info rights.
- **Path to liquidity** — realistic exit routes and horizon; plan for illiquidity.
- **Risk** — the 2–3 things most likely to zero it; run a pre-mortem
  (`skills/decision/stress-test.md`).

## Sizing

A **speculative, illiquid bucket** outside the public sleeves. Size so a total loss is survivable;
never fund it from money earmarked for the tradable account.

> **Privacy:** term sheets and founder data are confidential. Keep specific deals out of any repo
> you intend to publish.
