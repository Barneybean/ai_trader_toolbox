# Software / AI / internet — playbook

**What sets the price:** durable revenue growth, **net revenue retention (NRR)**, gross margin,
**Rule of 40** (growth + FCF margin), and now **AI monetization** (is AI adding revenue or just
cost?). Watch **stock-based comp** dilution and true FCF.

**Catalysts to hunt (+ source):** new-product/AI-feature attach and pricing; large enterprise/gov
contracts (**RPO/backlog**); margin-inflection (efficiency); guidance revisions; compute capex
cycles. For AI infra, the value-chain read: who's the picks-and-shovels beneficiary of a capex
wave (`skills/edge/thematic-waves.md`) (transcripts, 10-Q, partner announcements, hyperscaler capex guides).

**Valuation:** EV/S vs. growth, EV/FCF, DCF; **normalize for SBC**. Rerate on the revision trend.

**Red flags:** growth decel with no margin offset; SBC masking GAAP losses; "AI narrative" with no
monetization; customer concentration; long-duration/rate-sensitive multiple compression.

**Primary sources:** transcripts (RPO, NRR, guidance tone), 10-Q, customer/partner announcements,
hyperscaler capex guides.

**Variant angle:** real AI revenue attach the market lumps in with hype; an NRR/margin inflection
before it shows in prints; a platform quietly becoming the aggregator of a category.
