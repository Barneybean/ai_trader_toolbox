# Robotics / autonomy / hardware-services — playbook

The convergence layer (compute + power + models → physical world): humanoids, autonomy, drones,
delivery robots (SERV-type).

**What sets the price:** deployment scale (units/fleet), **unit economics** (contribution per
unit/delivery), commercial contracts, cash runway to scale. Mostly optionality on a scaling curve.

**Catalysts to hunt (+ source):** fleet/deployment expansion, new commercial partnerships,
unit-economics inflection to positive, funding rounds (transcripts, partnership press releases).

**Valuation:** EV/S on a steep-growth curve with a scaling-path narrative; heavy optionality —
flag it and size as a convex bet (`skills/decision/review-rubric.md` asymmetric sizing).

**Red flags:** cash burn vs. runway; deployment slower than promised; unit economics still
negative at scale.

**Variant angle:** a unit-economics inflection the market can't see yet; a category-defining
platform (`skills/edge/thematic-waves.md`) mispriced as a hardware vendor.
