# Nuclear / power / clean energy — playbook

Reactors/SMRs, utilities/IPPs, and storage — increasingly the *physical bottleneck* of the AI
buildout (`skills/edge/thematic-waves.md`).

**What sets the price:**
- **Pre-revenue reactor/SMR** (OKLO, SMR, NNE): regulatory milestones (**NRC licensing** stage),
  first-power/commercialization **timeline**, order backlog/LOIs/PPAs, funding & **dilution
  runway**, DOE support.
- **IPPs / utilities** (VST): power-demand growth (**AI data-center load**), PPA/capacity pricing,
  generation mix, rate base.
- **Storage** (FLNC): bookings/backlog, margins, project pipeline.

**Catalysts to hunt (+ source):** NRC design-approval/licensing steps, first-fuel/first-power
dates, DOE awards/loan guarantees, hyperscaler power-purchase deals, PPA signings, backlog
step-ups (NRC dockets, DOE announcements, transcripts, PPA press releases).

**Valuation:** pre-revenue names on **risk-adjusted DCF of the order book / capacity pipeline**
(mostly optionality — say so); utilities on EV/EBITDA, P/E, dividend, rate base + demand growth.

**Red flags:** perpetual "first power is 2–3 years away"; dilution to fund pre-revenue burn;
regulatory slippage; backlog that's LOIs, not firm orders.

**Primary sources:** NRC dockets, DOE announcements, earnings transcripts (backlog, timeline),
PPA press releases.

**Variant angle:** AI-driven power demand repricing "boring" utilities; a licensing milestone or
hyperscaler PPA the market hasn't connected to a specific name.
