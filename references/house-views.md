# House Views (starter template)

Your own **macro and technical heuristics** — the standing beliefs the desk should apply and cite.
This ships as a short generic starter; replace it with your actual views. Keep it opinionated and
dated; stale macro is worse than none.

## How to use it

List durable heuristics you trade by. The desk references these by name in recommendations
("applies house view: …"). Re-validate against live data — a heuristic is a prior, not a fact.

## Example heuristics (replace with your own)

- **Buy the washout that reclaims a key moving average** (e.g. price reclaiming the 30- or 50-day
  after a capitulation) rather than catching the falling knife.
- **High VIX = better entries.** Elevated fear widens the margin of safety; scale into quality when
  volatility spikes, don't freeze.
- **Respect the 200-day trend for the tape.** A great single-name chart in a market below its
  200-DMA still fights gravity — size accordingly.
- **Rates set the multiple.** Long-duration / unprofitable growth is rate-sensitive; own more of it
  when the rate path is easing, less when tightening.
- **Fade extremes in positioning**, not the primary trend — crowded-long and washed-out-hated are
  where reversals start.

> Add your own, delete what you don't believe, and cite them in the report so your reasoning is
> traceable.
